The Vision

The vision of M4 Empire is to build a community that offers affordable solutions to different human-related problems ranging from travel to business, transport, housing, charity, event planning and even cryptocurrency trading. The plan is to achieve these goals through the sale and purchase of NFTs (solidified crypto). NFT’s can be simply seen as solidified crypto. In the sense that the NFT’s can only be purchased on the blockchain with cryptocurrency. Automatically, when you buy an NFT token with your coin, you have solidified that coin at a certain price and so it will not be affected by the rise and fall of the actual coin. The moment an NFT is bought, your attention shifts from the coins to the tokens. That is, the NFT token you just bought. This is because profits can only be made on NFT’s by selling them back to the community that owns them or back to the blockchain that releases them. Now in most cases, no one knows the creators of these tokens because after they sell, they do not care about the customers and so therefore those tokens are bound to lose value, and for that those customers lose money. A Business NFT token, unlike regular tokens, has benefits from the companies that own the token. It automatically gives customers value for their money without selling the actual token itself. M4 empire has 21 companies under it and at the end of the year will be releasing 10 collections with a total of 50 tokens. 10 of our companies and 5 tokens to each company. Each token will have a different benefit from the other as every token will only have 100 pieces. And so, each benefit I announce as the year goes by and we finalize plans before the project launch in December will only be available to the first 100 people who purchase it when it launches. Every company will have the same base price for its tokens as the company's token is based off the company's credibility and the ability to deliver the said benefits. For now, I only have two examples I'm sure of, which I use to express my point, seeing as I am still working on organizing the benefits properly for all of them. The first one would be the Travel agency (M4 Travels). The base price for this company’s token is set at $300, and this token states that anyone that holds this token for exactly 1 year after purchase, gets an automatic 70% off any trip anywhere in the world with M4 Travels. The second would be the automobile company (M4 Autos). The base price at this company's token is $250, and this token states that anyone who can hold it for 7 months after purchase gets an automatic 35% off any car they want with M4 Autos. These are just two examples of which I am sure both tokens will be available at the launch. I am currently working on the remaining 48 benefits for the final 48 tokens. As I stated earlier, NFT’s are solidified crypto and so they only rise based off the ability of the creator to keep people interested in them (Basic Supply and Demand System), and this can only happen with the creator's ability to meet up with the benefits being promised. And that is the reason why business NFT’s should be thoroughly thought through as to not disappoint customers with failed promises.
